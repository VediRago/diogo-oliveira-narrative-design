# House Devaar

House Devaar is the later result of Teresta Ventari's rejected line returning to power through medicine, preservation, augmentation, and bodily dependency.

It does not belong fully to the Founding Era. Its roots begin there through Teresta's distance from House Ventari after learning the truth behind Viriatus's death.

Over generations, that rejected line survives through study, medicine, and delayed usefulness.

Much later, this path becomes House Devaar: a house whose power comes not from founding ideology, but from controlling the systems people physically depend on.
