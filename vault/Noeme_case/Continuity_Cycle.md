# Continuity Cycle

← Back to [Cosmology Reference](Cosmology_Reference.md)

The continuity cycle describes why Noeme cannot remain in total unity.

Experience requires difference. Difference requires separation. Separation produces fragments.

The cycle works like this:

Fragmentation creates difference. Difference creates experience. Experience creates connection. Connection moves toward unity. Unity becomes unstable because stable unity removes difference. Instability leads to re-fragmentation, which allows experience to continue.

This cycle explains why [Qalm](Qalm.md) requires separation and why [Unity](Unity.md) cannot remain stable forever.

The tragedy is that characters suffer the cycle as personal loss before anyone understands the structure behind it.
