# History Level — Era Timeline

## Purpose

Shows the History Level holding across a much longer span than one calendar's origin — atmosphere hardening into institutional history over three thousand years, not just one founding event.

## Core Principle

History Level doesn't stop at "an event became a calendar." Atmosphere can keep hardening into new institutional forms indefinitely, as long as each new form is a genuine consequence of the pressure before it — never invented as a fresh idea disconnected from what came before.

## Explanation

AVD ("After Viriatus's Death") begins not at Viriatus's private death, but at the moment Ermerus publicly declares Regulatus and the official story wins — history beginning when atmosphere becomes doctrine, not when the private event occurred.

That same logic repeats at larger scale, era after era. Era Three ends with Vys's architecture shifting from pearl-like structures toward metal and concrete — material culture changing as a visible symptom of accumulated pressure. Era Four begins not with collapse but overgrowth: success outgrowing the institutions built to manage it, so the Scutis expand into every major city under the banner of "restoring order" — a policy born directly from atmosphere (fear of disorder) hardening into law.

Cybernetics follow the same pattern in two layers. Layer One (~2000 AVD) is purely functional — restoring the body. Layer Two, arriving with Devaar (~2330 AVD), turns the same technology into dependency: clinics, contracts, controlled access — survival now passing through an institution rather than being restored by one. The solar flare famine (~2500 AVD) doesn't create that dependency; it exposes power that already existed, making it unavoidable.

Each stage is a direct consequence of the one before it — success creating overgrowth, overgrowth creating enforcement, enforcement creating dependency, dependency creating crisis. Nothing is added because it sounded interesting; each new institutional layer only exists because the previous one's atmosphere demanded it.
